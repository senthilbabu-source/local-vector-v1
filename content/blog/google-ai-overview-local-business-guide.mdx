## AI Overview is the new "position zero"

Google AI Overview — the AI-generated summary that appears above traditional search results — is now the most prominent element on a Google search results page for a growing number of queries.

For local searches like "best pizza near me" or "dentist open Saturday," AI Overview doesn't just provide a link to click. It synthesizes an answer from multiple sources and presents it as a definitive response. That's powerful when it's accurate — and devastating when it's not.

## How AI Overview works for local queries

When a user searches for a local business or service, Google's Gemini model:

1. **Pulls data from multiple sources** — Google Business Profile, your website, review platforms, directories, news articles
2. **Synthesizes a summary** — combines information into a conversational answer
3. **Presents it above all other results** — the AI answer is the first thing users see
4. **Cites sources selectively** — some sources are linked, many are used without citation

The key insight: **AI Overview doesn't just link to your website. It tells users about your business.** And what it tells them may not be what you'd tell them.

## The local business problem

Traditional SEO focused on getting your website to rank. AI Overview changes the game because:

- **Users may never visit your website** — the AI answer provides enough information to make a decision
- **Your GBP is not the only input** — AI pulls from review sites, social media, and cached web pages
- **Errors compound** — a wrong hours entry on one platform can override your correct GBP hours
- **There's no "edit" button** — you can't directly control what AI Overview says about you

## What you can control

While you can't edit AI Overview directly, you can influence what it says:

### Keep data consistent everywhere
AI Overview weighs consistency. If your website, GBP, Yelp, and Apple Maps all agree on your hours, that's what AI will display. If they disagree, AI picks — and sometimes picks wrong.

### Add structured data
JSON-LD schema on your website gives Googlebot machine-readable facts. This is especially important for hours, menus, services, and pricing — the data points most prone to hallucination.

### Monitor Google Search Console
Google now shows AI Overview impressions in Search Console. While it won't show you what the AI said, it tells you which queries triggered an AI Overview for your business.

### Use an AI monitoring tool
Tools like LocalVector.ai monitor what AI models actually say about your business and compare it against your verified data. This catches errors before they cost you customers.

## The GSC connection

Google Search Console's AI Overview data is valuable but limited. It shows:
- Which queries triggered AI Overview mentions of your site
- Impression counts for AI Overview vs traditional results
- Click-through rates from AI Overview

What it doesn't show: **what AI Overview actually said.** You know you appeared, but not whether the information was accurate. That's the gap that AI monitoring tools fill.

## Looking ahead

AI Overview is expanding to more query types and more markets. Google has signaled that AI-generated answers will become the default for most searches within the next few years. Local businesses that optimize for AI accuracy now will have a significant advantage.
