## The shift from search results to AI answers

For two decades, local businesses optimized for one thing: ranking on Google's first page. That game is changing. AI assistants like ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Copilot don't show ten blue links — they give one answer. And that answer either includes your business or it doesn't.

**Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)** is the practice of ensuring AI assistants accurately represent and recommend your business. It's not a replacement for SEO — it's a new channel that operates on different rules.

## How AI answers differ from search results

When someone Googles "best dentist in Atlanta," they see a list of options and choose. When someone asks ChatGPT the same question, they get a curated recommendation — often just one or two businesses — with specific claims about hours, services, and quality.

The critical difference: **AI states things as facts.** If ChatGPT says your restaurant is "permanently closed" or your dental clinic "doesn't accept new patients," users don't question it. They move on.

## The three pillars of AEO

### 1. Accuracy

AI models pull from dozens of sources: your website, Google Business Profile, Yelp, TripAdvisor, and more. When these sources disagree — and they often do — AI picks one. AEO starts with ensuring your data is consistent everywhere.

### 2. Visibility

Being accurate isn't enough if AI doesn't know you exist. AI Share of Voice measures how often your business appears in relevant AI queries. If your competitor shows up for "best pizza near me" and you don't, you've already lost the customer.

### 3. Framing

Even when AI mentions your business, it might frame you wrong. A fine-dining restaurant described as "affordable casual dining" loses its target customer. AEO includes managing how AI positions your brand.

## What you can do today

1. **Run an AI audit** — find out what ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity currently say about your business
2. **Fix data inconsistencies** — ensure your hours, address, phone, and services match across all platforms
3. **Add structured data** — JSON-LD schema on your website helps AI crawlers understand your business
4. **Create an llms.txt file** — give AI models a single source of verified truth about your business
5. **Monitor regularly** — AI models retrain frequently, so errors can resurface even after correction

## The bottom line

AEO isn't optional for local businesses in 2026. The businesses that get AI accuracy right will capture the growing share of customers who rely on AI assistants for local discovery. The ones that don't will lose revenue to competitors who do — or worse, to AI hallucinations that drive customers away.
